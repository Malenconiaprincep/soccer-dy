# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Douyin mini-game players who enter for short, repeatable football matches. They want fast decisions, immediate feedback, and a strong sense of competitive momentum on a vertical phone screen.

## Product Purpose

This product is a Cocos Creator football card and match mini-game. It should make lineup preparation, matchmaking, live match events, rewards, and progression feel sharp, physical, and worth replaying.

## Brand Personality

Competitive, explosive, premium sports.

The interface should feel like a live football broadcast crossed with a tactical squad room: energetic and high contrast, but controlled enough that players trust the state of the match.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic web-game gloss, noisy page-game decoration, template dashboards, excessive neon cyberpunk styling, childish cartoon softness, and cluttered reward screens where every element competes for attention.

## Design Principles

- Make every primary screen feel like match day, not a menu.
- Use motion to confirm state changes: searching, ready, kickoff, goals, rewards.
- Keep football information immediately scannable under thumb-driven play.
- Let premium assets carry emotion; avoid decorative clutter.
- Treat rewards and wins as impact moments, not static labels.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Maintain readable contrast for Chinese labels on mobile screens. Motion can be energetic but should remain short, directional, and state-driven rather than continuous visual noise.
